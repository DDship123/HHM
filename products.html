Zeniva Thermos

Technical Specifications of Zeniva Thermos Bottle – Durable, Safe, Environmentally Friendly

1. Capacity

  • 350ml / 500ml / 750ml / 1000ml (depending on product line)
  • Suitable design for students, office workers, gym goers and travelers.

2. Material

  • Body: Stainless steel 304 (high-grade stainless steel) – anti-corrosion, rust-free, safe for health.
  • Insulation layer: 2 vacuum layers + copper coating for optimal heat retention.
  • Lid: Virgin PP plastic, BPA free, combined with anti-leak silicone ring.

3. Heat retention

  • Keep warm: up to 12 hours
  • Keep refrigerated: up to 24 hours (Results may vary depending on usage environment and how often the lid is opened/closed.)

4. Design

  • Surface coated with electrostatic paint or anti-slip matte paint, easy to grip.
  • Modern design, elegant colors, suitable for minimalist living trend.
  • Light weight, easy to carry when going to school, work, picnics...

5. Size & Weight (500ml)

  • Height: ~22 cm
  • Bottom diameter: ~6.5 cm
  • Weight: ~300g

6.water disinfection

  • Zeniva thermos lid integrates UV rays to kill up to 99.9% of bacteria, ensuring water is always clean and safe after just a few minutes of exposure.

7. Additional features

  • Leak-proof lid – safe to keep in backpacks and handbags.
  • Some models come with tea strainers or reusable stainless steel straws.

Zeniva EcoBag

In the context of increasing concern for environmental protection, the use of natural products has become the priority choice of many people. Eco-friendly fabric bags for thermos bottles are a smart solution, helping you protect your thermos conveniently and contributing to reducing plastic waste.

Origin & Provenance

  • Products are designed and manufactured in Vietnam, by reputable craft manufacturers with a clear commitment to green - clean - safe production processes. We choose domestic materials to reduce transportation emissions and support local economic development.

Material

  • The bags are made from canvas, natural cotton or burlap, all of which are biodegradable, environmentally friendly and durable. These fabrics do not use toxic chemicals during processing, making them safe for the user's health.

Design style

  • The bag has a minimalist but sophisticated design, suitable for many users - from students to office workers or travel lovers. Neutral, gentle colors along with sturdy seams bring modernity and high aesthetics.

Utilities

  • Compact size, suitable for most popular thermos bottles on the market.
  • Convenient handle or strap, easy to carry when going to school, work or picnic.
  • Easy to wash, easy to clean, saving time and effort.
  • Good protection: Helps prevent scratches and reduce impact on the thermos when moving.

Benefit

  • Environmental protection: Minimize the use of nylon bags or plastic packaging.
  • Long-term cost savings: Durable material, can be reused many times.
  • Affirming a green lifestyle: Choosing a cloth bag is a small action but brings great value in spreading a positive lifestyle.
  • Meaningful product for gifts: With beautiful design, good quality and green message, the bag is very suitable as a corporate or personal gift.
