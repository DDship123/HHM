Zeniva – Thermos for a green environment

In the context of climate change, plastic pollution and increasingly depleted natural resources, choosing environmentally friendly products is no longer a trend, but has become an essential need. Realizing that, Zeniva was formed as a pioneer brand in providing green consumer products, with the desire to contribute to changing the daily living habits of Vietnamese people towards a more sustainable direction.

Zeniva was born not just to sell thermos bottles, but to inspire green living – every product we create carries the message: "Choose responsibly."

We believe that:

  • One reusable thermos can replace hundreds of single-use plastic bottles each year.
  • A smart consumer decision today can create a a more livable planet for future generations.
  • A small actionfrom each person will make a big change for the community.

Eco-Friendly Thermos – Small Choice, Big Meaning

In an era where plastic waste is rampant and climate change is a global issue, small changes in daily habits can make a significant positive impact. One simple yet effective solution is to use an eco-friendly thermos.

Thermos – Not just an item, but a green living declaration

Unlike disposable plastic bottles, thermos bottles are designed to be reused for a long time, helping to significantly reduce the amount of plastic waste in the environment. With the ability to keep hot/cold for many hours, you can bring water, coffee, tea or smoothies anytime, anywhere without having to buy additional bottled drinks - contributing to limiting unnecessary consumption.

Safe for your health – Healthy for the planet

Eco-friendly thermos bottles are usually made from 304 stainless steel, BPA-free – safe for users. In addition, many brands also use recycled materials or biodegradable packaging, reducing the impact on the ecosystem.

Positive impact on the environment

  • Reduce plastic waste and single-use products
  • Reducing emissions from plastic bottle/jar production
  • Promote a minimalist, sustainable lifestyle
  • Contribute to the protection of oceans, land and wildlife
